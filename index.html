After working with design and photography for a few years I studied Motion Design at Hyper Island. This led me to an internship at Animade, where I ended up working for almost three years, designing and animating for a range of clients and quite a few personal projects.

After moving back home to Sweden I’ve been lucky to work with more of my favourite studios such as Buck, Nexus and Oddfellows. Clients I've worked with include Google, Apple, Meta and Amazon.
I really enjoy teaching and have been guest lecturing at Hyper Island and Berghs in Sweden.

If you want to know more you can watch Adobe Live where I animate some walkcycles, or read an interview with WeTransfer about my personal project Words and Pals.
