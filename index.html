A bit more about me

I started off as an intern at Animade in London where I continued working for a few years before moving back to the forest in Småland, Sweden.

Since then I've been freelancing and have been lucky to work with more of my favourite studios such as Buck, Nexus and Oddfellows.
Clients I've worked with include Google, Apple, Meta and Amazon.
I enjoy teaching and have been guest lecturing at Hyper Island and Berghs in Sweden.

I do all kinds of 2D animation work – I work mainly in AfterEffects but anything goes. You can also hire me as a Designer, Illustrator or Animation Lead.

If you want to know more you can watch me animate some walkcycles at Adobe Live, or read an interview with WeTransfer about my personal project Words and Pals.
